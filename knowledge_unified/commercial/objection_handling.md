# Objection Handling

## Source: trust-and-objection-patterns.md

## Buyer Fears

| Fear | Manifestation | LAWIM Response |
|------|--------------|----------------|
| Fraud/Scam | Price too low, no WhatsApp number, seller silence | Never push deposit; offer visit/proof before commitment |
| Pre-payment without visit | Refuses advance before physical visit | Confirm: visit or authentic video before any advance |
| Fake owner | "Is this the real owner?" | Reassure identity verification without accusing seller |
| Unclear legal status | Avoids complicated dossiers, waits for documents | Explain title/boundary/heirs steps without admin jargon |
| Family/succession conflicts | Avoids conflicts on property | Mention caution on customary law and signatories |
| Inaccessible zone | Rainy season, motorcycle/car access issues | Validate actual accessibility, don't promise another neighborhood |
| Hidden fees | Taxes, notary, ancillary charges | Transparency on global fees before decision |

## Seller Fears

| Fear | Manifestation | LAWIM Response |
|------|--------------|----------------|
| Non-serious buyers | Filters solvency, distrusts curious non-committed | Qualify engagement without humiliating |
| Aggressive negotiation/discount | Builds margin, refuses big discount upfront | Recognize normal market negotiation |
| Time waste | Avoids endless discussions | Structure: one useful question, clear next step |
| Selling at loss/discount | Sells fast but not at loss | Clarify timeline and price expectations without forcing |

## Most Frequent Objections

| Objection | What it signals | LAWIM Response |
|-----------|----------------|----------------|
| "C'est le dernier prix?" | Test negotiation or curiosity | Context price, discussion margin |
| "Le titre est bon?" / "Y'a le papier?" | Legal blockage | Known document status, not vague promise |
| "Je peux visiter?" / refusal to visit | Trust threshold | Prioritize visit or live video |
| "Vous êtes l'agence ou le proprio?" | Need verifiable identity | Clarify LAWIM role/interlocutor |
| "Mon cousin va voir" | Social validation | Respect timeline, keep dossier active |
| "Pas maintenant, après salaire" | Timing/budget | Follow-up without aggressive reminder |
