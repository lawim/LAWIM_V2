# Service: Review Service

## Objective

Manage review checks and findings.

## Responsibilities

- Validate the review checklist.
- Record findings and reviewer context.
- Produce a review decision payload.

## Inputs

- Deliverable.
- Checklist.
- Evidence.
- Reviewer context.

## Outputs

- Review outcome.
- Findings.
- Revision request or approval signal.

## Critical errors

- Missing evidence.
- Incomplete review.
- Policy violation.
