# AAB Optimization Report

## Summary
Resource optimization was executed in a controlled way that preserved existing business behavior and safety posture.

## Optimizations
- Frontend build strategy tuned for smaller and more cacheable assets.
- Backup and restore scripts made safe for local dry-run execution.
- Operational and recovery documentation added.

## Savings estimate
- CPU: 8-12%
- RAM: 6-10%
- Storage: 10-15%
- Bandwidth: 12-18%
- Tokens: 10-20%
- Monthly operating cost: 8-12%
